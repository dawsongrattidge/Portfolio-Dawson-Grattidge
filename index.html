What I do

Hardware

I specialize in low-power electronics design, focusing on the creation of robust and energy-efficient IoT devices. My experience spans end-to-end design, incorporating a variety of RF systems such as LTE, Wi-Fi, GPS, and NFC. This includes extensive DFM (Design for Manufacturability) knowledge, complemented by experience in utilizing Altium Designer for low-level development.

AI Automation

Within AI Automation, I have honed my skills in developing and leveraging AI chatbots designed to streamline business operations. These automated systems play a crucial role in enhancing productivity by taking over key business functions such as customer support, employee onboarding, and training. Through intelligent automation, I aim to transform routine tasks into automated workflows, thereby increasing efficiency.

Software

In the software space, my forte lies in crafting scripts tailor-made for low-power IoT devices. I leverage machine learning on the edge to maximize data value while reducing data transmission. This involves developing drivers for sensors and other key chips that form the backbone of IoT solutions. While my core expertise is in IoT and machine learning, I also have a solid foundation in web development—as exemplified by this very website.
